    About Me

    I'm an undergraduate formally pursuing things quite separate from visual arts, but drawing is precious to me. Sadly, nowadays I rarely draw and when I do draw, it feels like I'm just trying to prove I still can. I'm haunted by the thought that I'm not 'going anywhere' with my art. This website is an attempt to remedy that; I can't articulate exactly why I think it will help, but putting my creations out there, letting them be a little more free and a little more immortal on the digital plane, feels nice.
